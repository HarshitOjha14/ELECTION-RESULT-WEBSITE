Our Aim

ResultInfo is public elections resource. It covers all Lok Sabha elections since 1984.ResultInfo already has data on over 10 national elections and will be enriched with inputs from users. Even though election data is available online, it is not easily searchable or cannot be consumed in structured formats necessary for analysis.ResultInfo aims to become the open-source equivalent and Wikipedia of election information in India. Much of the raw data for the site was sourced from the public domain and then processed to make it useful for analysis.
